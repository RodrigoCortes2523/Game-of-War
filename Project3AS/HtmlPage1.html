Card Game for Two

A card game designed for two players to play.

Are you bored? Looking for something to do? Grab a friend and get ready to play the Card Game for Two! Players will draw and compare their hand of cards until the deck is all out! Whoever possesses the highest value at the end of the deck will win the game! Think you've got what it takes to be a Card Champion? Give it a shot!
